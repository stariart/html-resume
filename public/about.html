About Me

My name is Sara and I live and breathe in the city of Southampton, on the south coast of the UK. I love anything to do with art and design. Symmetry is a passion of mine, and I love anything to do with mandala's and sacred geometry.

I have been making no-code websites for a few years now, all self taught on various platforms, but I am currently studying coding, and LOVING the learning process so far!

I can't wait to show the world what I can do! and at the matured age of 45 years old, I still LOVE to learn! My family and loved ones call me a human sponge!

Hobbies & Interests

I very much enjoy travel, long road trips around the country or abroad. Art in many forms from physical painting and sketching to crochet & macrame, digital graphic design, photography and most recently self-taught video editing.
